How Prioritisation Works

1. The Coordinates (X, Y)

Every work item is plotted on the matrix based on two scores:

  • Y-Axis (Impact):
    • For Productivity items, this is the Productivity Impact score (1-5).
    • For Enabling items, this is the Potential Impact score (1-5).
  • X-Axis (Effort): This comes from the Effort (Time) slider (1-5), where 1 is "Quick Fix" and 5 is "Major Project".

2. The Quadrants

Strategic Bets (Yellow)
High Impact (>3) but High Effort (>3). Plan these carefully.
Top Priority (Green)
High Impact (>3) & Low Effort (≤3). Do these first.
Deprioritise (Red)
Low Impact & High Effort. Avoid these.
Quick Wins (Blue)
Low Impact (<3) but Low Effort (≤3). Good fillers.

3. Ranking Algorithm

The "Strategic Order" list uses a raw score to rank items (Highest First):

Score = Impact × Reach × Urgency × Effort

  • Productivity Bonus: Productivity items get a 1.1x multiplier applied to their final score.
